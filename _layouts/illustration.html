  • Colour

  • Iconography

  • The curve

  • Typography

  • Illustration

  • Tone of voice

  • Photography

Illustrations

We wanted a unique style for our illustrations: something that suited our ethos of being welcoming, and non-corporate.

To summarise our illustration style:

  • - White background
  • - Thick, bold lines
  • - A "drawn" and "human" style
  • - Use brand secondary colours to add colour
  • - We encourage colouring outside the lines to add more personality
  • - Make use of the blob to add interest
